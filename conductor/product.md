# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
A frontend skeleton on Next.js for a Genius analogue.

## Target Audience
- **Music Enthusiasts:** Users seeking deep dives into song lyrics and meanings.
- **Casual Listeners:** Users looking for quick lyrics while listening to songs.
- **Content Creators:** Contributors adding and annotating lyrics.

## Core Features
- **Lyrics Annotation:** Interactive highlight and explain functionality for lyrics.
- **Advanced Search:** Search by artist, album, lyric snippets, or user.

## Primary Goal
**Community Engagement:** Focus on user interaction, annotations, and discussions.

## Design Tone
**Modern & Sleek:** Minimalist, light-themed design focusing on content readability with a vibrant purple (indigo) accent color.
